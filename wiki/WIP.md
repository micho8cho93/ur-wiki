## Economic Models

### Free-to-Play with Cosmetics
- Skin/theme packs for boards, dice, and pieces — no gameplay impact
- One-time purchases or bundled cosmetic passes
- Seasonal cosmetic drops tied to tournaments or events

### Premium Tier / Subscription
- Ad-free experience + exclusive cosmetics
- Priority matchmaking (shorter queue times)
- Expanded challenge slots or historical stats
- Monthly or annual billing via in-app purchase (Expo / RevenueCat)

### Tournament Entry Fees
- Optional paid-entry tournaments with prize pools or exclusive rewards
- Free-entry tiers alongside paid tiers to preserve accessibility
- In-game currency (soft) vs. real-money entry distinction — legal considerations vary by region

### Soft Currency Loop
- Earn currency through matches, challenges, daily quests
- Spend on cosmetics, challenge rerolls, or tournament entries
- Avoid pay-to-win: no currency advantage in rated or ranked play

## Monetization Considerations

- **Platform cut:** Apple/Google take 30% (15% for small developers) — factor into pricing
- **Subscription fatigue:** One-time cosmetic purchases may convert better than subscriptions for a niche game
- **Tournament economics:** Prize pools attract competitive players but require escrow / legal review
- **Regional pricing:** Use platform-native pricing tiers (App Store pricing matrix) for emerging markets
- **Analytics prerequisite:** Need retention and session data before optimizing any monetization funnel

## Organic User Growth

### Core Loop Virality
- Post-match share card: "I just beat X in 18 moves — play me" with deep link to match invite
- Spectator mode already exists — shareable live-match links could drive acquisition
- Leaderboard embeds / screenshots for social media

### Community & Content
- Discord server as hub for competitive players, feedback, and announcements
- Short-form content: TikTok / Reels of highlight moments (unusual dice runs, close finishes)
- Historical angle: position the game as a revival of the world's oldest board game — PR hook

### Retention Mechanics (feeds organic word-of-mouth)
- Daily challenges with streak rewards — gives players a reason to return and talk about it
- Seasonal tournaments with brackets and results pages — shareable outcomes
- ELO rankings + public leaderboard — competitive players self-promote

### App Store Optimization (ASO)
- Keywords: "board game", "ancient game", "strategy", "2-player", "online multiplayer"
- Screenshots showing multiplayer action and tournament brackets
- Video preview emphasizing the historical story + modern polish

### Influencer / Creator Angle
- Board game YouTube / podcast audience is small but highly engaged
- Niche streamers (board game Twitch) > broad gaming influencers for this title
- Press kit targeting "interesting history" angle (oldest board game + mobile revival)
